1.2 Provisions in Law

Full-time or Part-time?

Assessing business activities for unemployment benefits.

Part-time Business

Eligibility

Entrepreneurs or self-employed can work part-time, similar to employment relationships.

Benefit Impact

May be eligible for an adjusted unemployment benefit.

The benefit application must show that they are able to engage in full-time employment.

Burden of Proof

Assessment

How to prove availability?

By working or studying full-time for at least 6 months simultaneously with the business activity.

The Reality

In practice, providing other evidence is difficult.

"Usually the benefit applicant is deemed as doing business full-time."

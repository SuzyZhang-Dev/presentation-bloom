Case Law 5 Work or Leisure?

Member of board in a company
(Finlex 271:2024)

The Facts

  • • A had been working occasionally at X Oy and was a member of its board.
  • • Ownership: A`s spouse owned the shares of the company 100%.
  • • Claim: After terminating employment A claimed for labour market subsidy.
  • • The benefit was refused because A was deemed an entrepreneur.

The Court's Decision

The Court repealed the decision stating that A was not working as an entrepreneur.

Reasoning: A did not have any shares of X Oy and therefore did not excercise stewardship in business nor could participate company shareholders`meetings. However A had been in employment relationship.

A may have had right to adjusted benefit

The Core Principle

"Can a jobseeker work without pay without losing their unemployment benefits?"

Case Law Work or Leisure?

Work Without Pay | Rules & Risks

The General Rule

Work without remuneration at companies and businesses is strictly not allowed.

*Activities typically carried out in employment relationships cannot be done for free.

Risks & Consequences

  • • Disguised Employment: Authorities may deem the activity as employment or entrepreneurship.
  • • Benefit Impact: Immediate suspension of daily allowance or total ineligibility.

Allowed Exceptions

Unpaid work is permitted in non-profit organizations (common good) or as occasional mutual help (e.g., helping a neighbor).

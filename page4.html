Provisions in Law

What activities can a jobseeker take part in
without losing unemployment benefits?

Allowed

Safe Activities

Non-profit Organizations

Activities without pay in common-good non-profit organizations.

Mutual Help

Occasional common-good work helping others (e.g., neighbour's renovation).

Not Allowed

Risk to Benefits

Unpaid Corporate Work

Work without remuneration at companies and businesses geared towards profit.

Disguised Employment

Work activities that are normally carried out in paid employment relationships.

The Core Question

"Is the player playing for profit or passion? Where is the line drawn?"

Case Law Work or Leisure?

Ice Hockey Player | Pro or Amateur?

Key Assessment Factors

  • Status: Contracted/professional match fees vs. amateur status.
  • Schedule: Intensity of training and match schedule.
  • Income: Regularity and amount of earnings.

Scenario A: Professional / Paid

If the player receives regular remuneration or match fees, it is typically deemed as self-employment or professional work.

Impacts benefits (Full/Part-time)

Scenario B: Amateur Status

Players with amateur status who receive only small stipends or expense allowances are usually not considered to be working.

Likely retains full benefits

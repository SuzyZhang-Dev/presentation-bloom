Case Law 7 Work or Leisure?

Twitch online gamer
(Finlex 2535:2023)

The Facts

  • • During unemployment A engaged in online gaming through Twitch platform.
  • • A would stream the games and the audience would give credit points. The credit points were converted into payments in crypto assets.
  • • Income: Occasional payments were worth some 100 euro/month.
  • • Time: The time spent online was about 4 hours/day in 5 days/week.
  • • Refusal: A`s claim for labour market subsidy was denied because A`s activity constituted entrepreneurship.

The Court's Decision

The Court did not change the decision.

Note:

The Court took a vote. According to dissenting opinion gaming was only a past-time activity that did not pose a hindrance to accept full-time employment.

The Core Question

"Does the board position involve executive power or is it merely passive oversight?"

Case Law Work or Leisure?

Company Board | Member Role

Key Assessment Factors

  • • Remuneration: Salary vs. meeting fees vs. unpaid.
  • • Control & Ownership: Shareholding percentage and voting power.
  • • Nature of Tasks: Strategic management vs. passive oversight.

Assessment: Deemed as Work

If the role involves remuneration, active strategic participation, or significant control, it is likely considered entrepreneurship or self-employment.

Affects benefits (Adjusted/Denied)

Assessment: Less Likely Work

A minimal, unpaid, and purely passive oversight role is generally not considered work and is less likely to impact unemployment benefits.

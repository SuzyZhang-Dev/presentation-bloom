The Core Question

"When does a creative video hobby transform into a professional business activity?"

Case Law Work or Leisure?

YouTube Activity | Hobby or Work?

Key Factors Assessed

  • • Monetization (Ads, Sponsors)
  • • Upload Frequency
  • • Marketing Intent
  • • Audience Size
  • • Time Investment (Filming/Editing)

Assessment Trends

Scenario A: Hobby

No ads, occasional uploads, no growth plan

Likely Not Work

Scenario B: Pro

Monetized, regular schedule, intent to grow

Self-Employment

Impact on Benefits

If deemed self-employment, it may be classified as full-time or part-time based on the time required, directly affecting eligibility for unemployment allowances.

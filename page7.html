Background

"Unemployed A had been making Youtube videos since 2008."

Case Law 2 Work or Leisure?

Youtube activity

The Facts & Claim

  • • A claimed for labor market subsidy in 2022.
  • • Stated that the activity was only a hobby as well as a means to enhance job searching.
  • • Time: In some weeks A would not engage in Youtube at all, while in other weeks A would work even 18 hours/day.
  • • Income: A did receive some income from Youtube, but never more than 300 euro/month.

The Court's Decision

The Court held that A was a full time entrepreneur.

Despite the hours spent on the activity varied over time, in the long run its continuance would indicate that the activity was not to be classified as a hobby.

A did not have right to the benefit

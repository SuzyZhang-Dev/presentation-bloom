Case Law 4 Work or Leisure?

Member of board at association (ry)

The Facts

  • • A had been working at a common-good association between 1.1.-31.10. as a salaried manager. A was also chair of the board.
  • • From 1.11. A continued merely as chair.
  • • Claim: A was denied of earnings related daily allowance because A was deemed working as an entrepreneur from that moment.

The Court's Decision

The Court held that legislation in this case did not support the view that A would be employed through entrepreneurship.

The fact that a person is only a member of the board does not consitute entrepreneurship since the aim of an association is not to generate economic profit.

Therefore A was entitled to the benefit as of 1.11.
